On this, 12th day of the 7th Mongol lunar month, died the greatest of the military leaders of all times Chingiz KhaтАЩKhan. Born Temujin, he literally rose from rags after his father was killed by Tatars, and was elected KhaтАЩKhan in 1206 after being tied down in Mongolian tribal wars till age 40.
