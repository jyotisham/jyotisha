### рддрд░рд╛рдЗрди-рдпреБрджреНрдзреЗ рдкреГрдереНрд╡реАрд░рд╛рдЬ-рдЬрдпрдГ #реорейрез

Event occured on 1191-11-20 (gregorian). Julian date was converted to Gregorian in this reckoning. 

chAhamAna-pRthvIrAja (Prithiviraj Chauhan) defeated the Ghurid king Mu'izz ad-Din Muhammad Ghori. 'The Sultan turned his charger's head round and receded, and from the agony of the wound he was unable to continue on horseback any longer. Defeat befell the army of Isl─Бm so that it was irretrievably routed.' - Minhaj in Tabaqat-i Nasiri.

Prithviraj later recaptured Tabarhindah fort, but did not pursue the enemy further.

Context: Sometime before 1191, Mu'izz ad-Din's army captured the Tabarhindah fort (probably present-day Bathinda).
