Hassan Nizami (the famous sufi of Chishti order, of Nizamuddin Auliya's known silsil├г) was indulging in frenzied anti-hindu activity - including conversions in Central Provinces.  Sita Ram Goel writes:

Codename of Rajguru in this operation was "M" - "M" for Maratha. Veer Savarkar had used same pseudonym to write his "Hindutva" year before.
